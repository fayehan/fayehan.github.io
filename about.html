Hi, I'm Faye!

I am a product designer in my 4th year at UC Berkeley studying Computer Science, Cognitive Science and Design Innovation. In the past I've designed at Evernote and Intuit. Currently, I'm doing UX research for Wearables in the Internet of Things in the BEST lab. I also help run Berkeley Innovation, a student-run design consultancy.

Passionate about creating delightful experience and crafting innovative products, I believe that good designs always start with the needs of people. When I am not studying or designing, you will catch me exploring cafés, collecting Japanese stationery, taking film photographs, or munching on dried mangoes.
